![Group Photo of Choir members](https://trailharmonychoir.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/2f2d1-cropped-harmonybannerphoto_cr.png) ## **_Welcome to our Trail Harmony Choir!_** The Trail Harmony Choir is a non-auditioned mixed community choir in Trail, BC. Our repertoire covers sacred music, Broadway, jazz and contemporary, both with and without accompaniment. We practice weekly from September through April and perform at local events and our annual Spring Concert.  The startup for the Fall season will be a Wednesday in September at Trail United Church @ 7:00pm.  We would love to have you join us!
